## Content Creation Tips

**For Pinterest:**
- Vertical images (1000x1500px)
- Text overlay with key info
- Bright, eye-catching colors
- Clear product photos
- Watermark with ShelzyPerkins.com

**For Instagram:**
- High-quality, well-lit photos
- Cohesive aesthetic
- Show products in use
- Use all 30 hashtags
- Post at optimal times (7am, 12pm, 6pm)

**For TikTok:**
- Vertical video (9:16 ratio)
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Show product in action
- Authentic, not overly produced
- Trending audio when relevant

**For Facebook:**
- Mix of links and native content
- Ask questions to drive engagement
- Post when your audience is most active
- Use Facebook Groups strategically

**For Email:**
- Personal, conversational tone
- Clear subject lines
- Mobile-optimized
- Single clear CTA
- Send consistently (weekly)

---

**Pro Tip:** Batch-create content! Spend 2-3 hours once a week creating all social media content, then schedule it out. This is more efficient than creating daily.
