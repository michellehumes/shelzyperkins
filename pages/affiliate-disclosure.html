In the interest of full transparency, I want to be upfront about how ShelzyPerkins.com makes money and how this might affect the content you see.

The Short Version

ShelzyPerkins.com contains affiliate links. If you click on a product link and make a purchase, I may receive a small commission at no extra cost to you. This is how I support the site and keep bringing you great content!

Amazon Associates Disclosure

ShelzyPerkins.com is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com.

As an Amazon Associate, I earn from qualifying purchases. This means:

  • When you click on a link to Amazon from this site, it may be an affiliate link
  • If you make a purchase within a certain timeframe after clicking, I may earn a commission
  • The price you pay is the same whether you use my affiliate link or not
  • You will never pay more because you clicked through my site

How This Affects My Content

Here's what I want you to know about how I approach affiliate content:

What I WILL Do:

  • Only recommend products I genuinely believe are worth your money
  • Clearly disclose when posts contain affiliate links
  • Share my honest opinion, even about products I'm affiliated with
  • Update prices when I notice they've changed significantly
  • Remove or note products that are no longer available

What I WON'T Do:

  • Recommend a product just because it has a high commission
  • Hide the fact that I earn from affiliate links
  • Exaggerate product benefits to make a sale
  • Recommend products I haven't researched or don't believe in

Other Potential Partnerships

In addition to Amazon Associates, I may occasionally:

  • Partner with brands for sponsored content (always clearly labeled)
  • Receive products for review purposes
  • Use affiliate links from other retailers

Any sponsored content or gifted products will be clearly disclosed in the relevant post.

Why I Use Affiliate Links

Running a website costs money – hosting, domain fees, email services, and countless hours of research and content creation. Affiliate commissions help me:

  • Keep the site running and ad-free (or with minimal ads)
  • Invest in better tools and resources for finding deals
  • Spend more time creating helpful content for you
  • Purchase products to test and review

When you use my affiliate links, you're directly supporting this site at no cost to yourself. Thank you!
