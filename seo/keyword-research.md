## SEO Content Guidelines

### Title Formula (Best Performing)
1. Number + Adjective + Product Category + Under $Price
   - Example: "15 Life-Changing Amazon Products Under $30"

2. "Best" + Product Category + Qualifier
   - Example: "Best Amazon Beauty Dupes That Actually Work"

3. Product Category + "That" + Benefit
   - Example: "Amazon Kitchen Gadgets That Look High-End"

4. Number + Product Category + "Your" + Target Audience + Will Love
   - Example: "20 Amazon Products Your Dog Will Obsess Over"

### Meta Description Formula
- Hook (create curiosity)
- Benefit (what they'll get)
- Call-to-action
- Under 155 characters

**Good Example:**
"Discover 15 Amazon beauty dupes that look and perform like luxury brands for a fraction of the price. Save hundreds without sacrificing quality!"

### Content Structure (Every Post)
1. **Introduction** (150-200 words)
   - Hook in first sentence
   - Promise the benefit
   - Include focus keyword

2. **Table of Contents** (for 2000+ word posts)
   - Improves user experience
   - Reduces bounce rate

3. **Affiliate Disclosure** (before first link)
   - FTC compliant
   - Use shortcode: `[affiliate_disclosure]`

4. **Product Sections** (800-1500 words)
   - Use `[product_card]` shortcodes
   - Include H2 and H3 subheadings
   - Focus keyword in at least one H2

5. **Comparison Table** (for list posts)
   - Use `[comparison_table]` shortcode
   - Makes content scannable

6. **FAQ Section** (5-10 questions)
   - Targets featured snippets
   - Long-tail keyword opportunities

7. **Conclusion** (100-150 words)
   - Summarize key points
   - Strong call-to-action

8. **Email Signup Form**
   - Use `[email_signup]` shortcode
   - Offer relevant lead magnet

### Word Count Targets
- Minimum: 1,500 words
- Ideal: 2,000-3,000 words
- Comprehensive guides: 3,500-5,000 words

## Keyword Density Guidelines

**Focus Keyword:**
- Title: Yes (required)
- URL: Yes (required)
- First 100 words: Yes (required)
- H2 subheadings: 1-2 times
- Throughout content: 0.5-1.5% density
- Meta description: Yes (required)
- Image alt text: 1-2 times

**Related Keywords (LSI):**
Include naturally throughout content:
- Synonyms of focus keyword
- Related product categories
- Common search phrases
- Question-based long-tails

## Link Building Strategy

### Internal Linking (Critical for SEO)
- Every post links to 3-5 related posts
- Use descriptive anchor text (not "click here")
- Link from high-traffic posts to new posts
- Create content clusters (all related posts link together)

### External Linking (Trust Signals)
- Link to 1-2 authority sites per post
- Examples: Consumer Reports, Good Housekeeping, Real Simple
- Opens in new tab
- rel="nofollow" when appropriate

### Affiliate Links (Revenue)
- 10-30 affiliate links per post
- Multiple links to top products
- Mix of product cards and text links
- All use correct affiliate tag: shelzysdesigns-20
