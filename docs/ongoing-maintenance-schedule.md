# ShelzyPerkins.com - Ongoing Maintenance Schedule

## Daily Tasks (15-20 minutes)

### Morning Routine (10 minutes)
- [ ] **Check Amazon for hot deals**
  - Visit: amazon.com/goldbox
  - Look for deals with 40%+ off
  - Products with high ratings (4.5+ stars)
  - Items with limited time offers

- [ ] **Update Daily Deals post**
  - Replace yesterday's deals with today's
  - Update prices and savings percentages
  - Add "TODAY ONLY" urgency badges

- [ ] **Schedule Pinterest pins** (15 pins/day)
  - Pin to relevant boards
  - Use templates from `/automation/pinterest/pin-templates.json`
  - Best times: 7am, 12pm, 3pm, 6pm, 9pm

### Evening Routine (5-10 minutes)
- [ ] **Check affiliate dashboard**
  - Log into Amazon Associates
  - Check today's clicks and earnings
  - Note which posts are performing well

- [ ] **Respond to comments**
  - Reply to blog comments
  - Answer questions
  - Thank readers for engaging

- [ ] **Monitor analytics**
  - Quick check of Google Analytics
  - Note any traffic spikes
  - Check for any errors

## Weekly Tasks (3-4 hours)

### Monday: Content Planning (45 minutes)
- [ ] **Plan this week's posts** (Target: 3 posts)
  - Choose topics from content calendar
  - Research keywords
  - Gather product ASINs
  - Create outlines

- [ ] **Check trending products**
  - Browse "Amazon Best Sellers"
  - Check "Movers & Shakers" (biggest gainers)
  - Note viral products on TikTok/Pinterest

- [ ] **Review last week's performance**
  - Which posts got most traffic?
  - Which products generated most clicks?
  - What should we double down on?

### Tuesday: Content Creation Day 1 (2 hours)
- [ ] **Write Post #1**
  - 1500-3000 words
  - 10-30 affiliate links
  - Product cards for main items
  - Comparison table (if list post)
  - SEO optimized (Rank Math 80+)

- [ ] **Create Pinterest pins** (3-5 per post)
  - Use Canva templates
  - Brand colors: #FF6B6B, #FFE66D
  - Vertical format: 1000x1500px
  - Include product images and text overlay

- [ ] **Publish and promote**
  - Schedule post for optimal time
  - Share to Pinterest immediately
  - Add internal links from old posts

### Wednesday: Content Creation Day 2 (2 hours)
- [ ] **Write Post #2**
  - Follow Tuesday's process
  - Different category from Post #1
  - Mix of product types and price points

- [ ] **Update old posts**
  - Refresh top 3 performing posts from last month
  - Add new products
  - Update prices
  - Check for broken links
  - Update images if needed

### Thursday: Content Creation Day 3 (2 hours)
- [ ] **Write Post #3**
  - Complete week's content goal
  - Ensure variety in topics

- [ ] **Email newsletter prep**
  - Draft this week's newsletter
  - Feature top 3-5 deals
  - Highlight new post
  - Personal intro paragraph

### Friday: Promotion & SEO (1 hour)
- [ ] **Send weekly newsletter**
  - Subject line with emoji and urgency
  - Best time: 10am or 2pm
  - Include clear CTAs

- [ ] **Social media promotion**
  - Share this week's posts on all channels
  - Create Instagram stories (if applicable)
  - Post to Facebook page

- [ ] **SEO check**
  - Submit new posts to Google Search Console
  - Check for indexing issues
  - Monitor keyword rankings

### Weekend: Analytics & Planning (30 minutes)
- [ ] **Deep dive into analytics**
  - Google Analytics: Traffic sources, top posts
  - Amazon Associates: Clicks, conversion rate, earnings
  - Pinterest Analytics: Top pins, engagement
  - Email stats: Open rate, click rate

- [ ] **Plan next week's content**
  - Based on this week's performance
  - Seasonal opportunities
  - Trending topics

## Emergency Maintenance (As Needed)

### When Amazon Affiliate Link Stops Working
1. Check Amazon Associates account status
2. Verify affiliate tag correct (shelzysdesigns-20)
3. Test in incognito mode
4. Contact Amazon Associates support if needed

### When Site Goes Down
1. Check GoDaddy status
2. Clear all caches
3. Deactivate plugins one by one
4. Contact GoDaddy support: 1-480-505-8877

### When Traffic Drops Suddenly
1. Check Google Search Console for issues
2. Verify site is still indexed (Google: `site:shelzyperkins.com`)
3. Check if penalized or manual action
4. Review recent changes that might have caused drop

### When Earning Drops Without Traffic Drop
1. Check affiliate links still working
2. Verify products still available/in stock
3. Review conversion rate in Amazon Associates
4. Test user experience (slow site? broken forms?)

## Automation Opportunities

To reduce time investment, automate:

1. **Pinterest scheduling** - Use Tailwind ($14.99/mo)
   - Schedule all pins for the week in 30 minutes
   - Auto-schedule to optimal times
   - Reduces daily task from 15 min to 0

2. **Deal finding** - Use deal automation script
   - File: `automation/deals/deal-automation.js`
   - Automatically fetches daily deals
   - Reduces daily task from 10 min to 2 min

3. **Email sequences** - Pre-write and automate
   - Welcome sequence runs on autopilot
   - Weekly newsletter template speeds up writing
   - Reduces weekly task from 45 min to 15 min

4. **Social sharing** - Use social media scheduler
   - Buffer or Hootsuite free tier
   - Schedule week's posts in advance
   - Reduces weekly task from 30 min to 10 min

5. **Analytics reporting** - Use Google Data Studio
   - Auto-generate monthly reports
   - Visual dashboards
   - Reduces monthly task from 60 min to 15 min

**With automation:** Cut time investment by 50% (150-200 hours/year)
**Same revenue:** $12,000-30,000/year
**New effective hourly rate:** $60-150/hour
