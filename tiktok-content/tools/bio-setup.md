## Link in Bio Options

### Option 1: Free - Google Form Direct Link
Just paste your Google Form URL directly in bio.

**Pros:** Free, simple
**Cons:** Looks unprofessional, can't track clicks

### Option 2: Free - Linktree
1. Go to [linktr.ee](https://linktr.ee)
2. Create free account
3. Add these links:
   - Order Form (Google Form)
   - Amazon Storefront (if you have one)
   - Instagram
   - Contact/Email

**Sample Linktree Setup:**
```
SHOP NOW - Order your bottle
$25 + shipping | 6 colors available
[Google Form Link]

SUPPLIES I USE
Everything from my videos
[Amazon Storefront Link]

FOLLOW ME
More content daily
[Instagram Link]
```

### Option 3: Paid - Stan Store ($29/mo)
Best option once you're making consistent sales.

**Setup:**
1. Go to [stan.store](https://stan.store)
2. Sign up with TikTok
3. Create product:
   - Title: "Personalized Water Bottle"
   - Price: $25
   - Add photo of your bottles
   - Add order form questions
4. Connect payment (Stripe)
5. Add link to TikTok bio

**Stan Store Advantages:**
- Takes payment directly
- Built-in order form
- Professional look
- Analytics
- Upsells

## DM Auto-Response Setup

TikTok doesn't have auto-responses, but you can:

1. **Pin a comment** on your videos:
```
HOW TO ORDER:
1. DM me "ORDER"
2. I'll send you the form
3. Fill it out + pay
4. Get your bottle in 3-5 days!
```

2. **Copy-paste response ready** (save in Notes app):
```
Hey! Thanks for reaching out!

To order:
Fill out this form: [LINK]
Send $30 to Venmo: @handle

I'll start on yours today!
```
