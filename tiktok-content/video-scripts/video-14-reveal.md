## Filming Checklist

- [ ] Cloth or fabric to cover initially

2. **Pull away cloth dramatically** (2 sec)
3. **Pan across all bottles** (4 sec)
4. **Close-up of each name** (1 sec each)
5. **Final wide shot** (2 sec)
