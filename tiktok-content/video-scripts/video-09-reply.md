# Video 9: "Replying to Comment - Yes I Sell These!"

**Format:** Reply to comment (faceless)
**Length:** 15-25 seconds
**Post Time:** Saturday 7pm
**Post Order:** 5th

---

## Hook (First 1-2 seconds)
Show the comment on screen: **"OMG are you selling these?!"**

---

## How to Create This Video

1. Wait until you get a comment asking "are you selling these?" or "how much?"
2. Screen record that comment
3. Film your response showing products/process
4. Post as a reply to that comment

**If you don't have comments yet:** Create a fake screenshot or use "Replying to @user" format

---

## Filming Checklist

- [ ] Screen record a real comment (or create one)
- [ ] Show your workspace with bottles
- [ ] Show color options available
- [ ] End with clear pricing and CTA

---

## What to Film (In Order)

1. **Screen record the comment** (2 sec)
   - Show the question being asked

2. **Your workspace with bottles** (4 sec)
   - Show you're a real business

3. **Color options available** (4 sec)
   - Quick cuts of each vinyl color

4. **Packaging process** (3 sec)
   - Show care and quality

5. **Finished bottle** (3 sec)
   - Final product shot

6. **"DM to order" end screen** (2 sec)
   - Clear call to action

## CapCut Editing Steps

1. Import comment screenshot/recording
2. Import product footage
3. Arrange in order: comment → response footage
4. Add text overlays at timestamps
5. Generate AI voiceover from script
6. Add subtle background music at 15% volume
7. Add auto-captions
8. Export 1080p 60fps

---

## Posting Strategy

**How to post as a reply:**
1. Go to the original comment on your video
2. Tap "Reply"
3. Tap the camera icon to reply with video
4. Upload your pre-made video

**This creates:** "Replying to @username" at the top of your video, which:
- Shows social proof
- Creates engagement
- Makes viewers curious about your other content
- Positions you as responsive and customer-focused
