**Format:** Color options showcase (faceless)

## Hook (First 1-2 seconds)

---

## Filming Checklist

- [ ] Same name "Emma" in 6 different vinyl colors
- [ ] Clean, consistent background
- [ ] Good lighting to show true colors
- [ ] Quick cuts between each color

---

## Colors to Show

Make the same bottle with "Emma" in each color:

1. **Hot Pink** - Bold/fiery personality
2. **Baby Pink** - Soft girl era
3. **Gold** - Main character energy
4. **Rose Gold** - Hopeless romantic
5. **Teal** - Creative/unique
6. **Black** - Edgy/mysterious

---

## What to Film

Film each bottle individually:
- Same angle for each
- Same background
- Same lighting
- 2-3 seconds per color
- Quick cuts between
