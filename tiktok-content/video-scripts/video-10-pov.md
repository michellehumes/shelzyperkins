---

## Filming Checklist

- [ ] Multiple finished bottles with different names
- [ ] Pretty display setup
- [ ] Cute tote bag or packaging
- [ ] Aesthetic vibes throughout

---

## What to Film (In Order)

1. **All bottles lined up beautifully** (3 sec)
   - Hero shot of all bottles together
   - Good lighting, clean background

2. **Close-up pan across all names** (4 sec)
   - Slow pan left to right
   - Show each name clearly

3. **Placing bottles into a bag** (3 sec)
   - Cute tote bag or gift bag
   - Careful, aesthetic placement

4. **Zipping/closing the bag** (2 sec)
   - Ready to go moment

5. **Final "ready to go" shot** (2 sec)
   - Bag packed, ready for the party
