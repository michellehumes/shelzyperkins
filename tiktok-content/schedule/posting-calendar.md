## Weekly Content Cycle

### Week 3+: Repeat & Expand

Once you've posted all 10, create variations:

**New Tutorial Ideas:**
- Different fonts (not just Pecita)
- Tumblers instead of bottles
- Different color combos
- Troubleshooting tips

**New Engagement Posts:**
- "Name that sells the most"
- "Most requested color"
- Customer reaction videos (screenshots)
- Before/after transformations

**Trending Content:**
- Jump on trending sounds
- Recreate viral formats with your bottles
- Duet/stitch other creators

---

## Content Batching Schedule

**Every Sunday: Plan**
- Check what performed well last week
- Note trending sounds
- Plan next 7 videos

**Every Wednesday: Film**
- Film 4-5 videos in one session
- Screen record tutorials
- Product shots and process videos

**Every Thursday: Edit**
- Edit all videos in CapCut
- Add voiceovers, text, sounds
- Save to drafts

**Daily: Post & Engage**
- Post scheduled video
- Engage for 15-30 min
- Reply to all comments
