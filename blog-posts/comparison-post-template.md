# Product Comparison Post Template

Use this template to create high-converting comparison posts. Comparison content has 3-5x higher conversion rates because readers are in decision mode.

---

## Post Structure

### Title Format Options:
- "[Product A] vs [Product B]: Which One Should You Buy in 2024?"
- "I Tested [Product A] and [Product B] - Here's the Winner"
- "[Product A] vs [Product B] Comparison: The Ultimate Guide"
- "Best [Category]: [Product A] vs [Product B] Showdown"

### SEO Meta Description (150-160 chars):
"Detailed [Product A] vs [Product B] comparison. We tested both to find the winner. See specs, pros/cons, and which one is worth your money."

## Pro Tips for Comparison Posts

1. **Always declare a winner** - Readers want a recommendation
2. **Include price history** - Use `[product_card_enhanced]` with lowest/highest prices
3. **Be specific about use cases** - "Product A is better for X, Product B for Y"
4. **Add personal experience** - "After testing for 2 weeks..."
5. **Update regularly** - Prices and availability change
6. **Use comparison tables** - Easy to scan, good for SEO
7. **Include both products in title** - Better for search
8. **Answer common questions** - FAQ section = featured snippets
